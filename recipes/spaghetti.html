Mom's Spaghetti

Nothing fills you up faster than a plate of mom's spaghetti and meat sauce with a slice or two of garlic bread on the side.



Ingredients

  • 2lbs Ground Beef
  • 2 Big Cans of Tomato Sauce
  • 2 Cans of Sliced Mushrooms
  • 2 Cans of Chopped Olives
  • 2 Large Green Bell Peppers
  • 1 Large Onion
  • Italian Seasoning
  • Choice of Pasta
  • Garlic Bread


Directions

  1. Cut onion and green bell peppers.

  2. Add onion, green peppers, mushrooms, olives, and Italian seasoning with the ground beef on a stove-top pot.

  3. Brown meat and begin to break it down into bite-size pieces.

  4. Once meat is cooked fully, add tomato sauce to pot and heat.

  5. Make pasta of choice and prepare garlic bread as needed.

  6. Serve & enjoy!!
