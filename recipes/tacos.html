Mom's Tacos

Mom's Tacos are a favorite at home for any season but they always bring the feeling of summer with them.



Ingredients

  • 2lbs Ground Beef
  • Mild Taco Sauce
  • 1 Medium Onion
  • 1 Can of Chopped Olives
  • 3 Tomatoes
  • 1 Bag Shredded Cheddar Cheese
  • 1 Bag Shredded Lettuce
  • Sour Cream
  • Taco Shells

Directions


  1. Chop onion and tomatoes, put tomatoes aside for later.
  2. Mix onion and olives in a stove-top pot with ground beef.
  3. Brown the meat. Once it's fully cooked, add mild taco sauce as needed for flavoring and heat.
  4. Use meat to fill taco shells.
  5. Use cheese, lettuce, tomatoes, and sour cream as toppers to the beef tacos.
