Poor Man's Casserole

Poor Man's Casserole is an affordable and easy casserole to make in a bind.



Ingredients

  • 2lbs Ground Beef
  • 1 Medium Onion
  • 1 Bag Shredded Cheddar Cheese
  • 1 Bag Frozen Broccoli
  • Frozen Tater Tots


Directions

  1. Chop onion & mix in with beef on a stove-top pot.
  2. Once meat is browned and fully cooked, take off heat.
  3. In a casserole dish, layer meat, cheese, and broccoli until all ingredients are used up.
  4. Top casserole dish with cheese and cover entirely with tater tots.
  5. Bake at 350 for 35min or as needed for a brown, crispy tater tot.
  6. Enjoy!
