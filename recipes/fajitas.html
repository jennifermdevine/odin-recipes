My Fajitas

My chicken fajitas have easy dump it and cook steps but produce great results.



Ingredients

  • 2lbs Chicken Breast
  • 2 Green Peppers
  • 2 Red Peppers
  • 2 Yellow Peppers
  • 1 Medium Onion
  • 1 Can Diced Tomatoes
  • 2 Limes
  • 1 Packet Fajita Seasoning
  • Sour Cream
  • Tortillas

Directions


  1. Chop all peppers & onion.
  2. Put chicken breasts in a crockpot & put peppers and onions on top.
  3. Drain can of diced tomatoes & add to crockpot.
  4. Mix in fajita seasoning to taste.
  5. Cut open limes and squeeze lime juice over top everything.
  6. Cook on low for 8hrs or high for 4hrs.

  7. Enjoy with tortillas!
